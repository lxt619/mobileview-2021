My works

My interest in visual design started in 2016, when I was a sophomore in high school, because I liked photography, I founded my own photography studio. Since then, I have slowly developed my own works.

Until I went to college, I found that graphic design is also very interesting, so I started to get in touch with illustration, poster design and so on. On the day I came into contact with UX/UI design, I knew what I was really interested in.
